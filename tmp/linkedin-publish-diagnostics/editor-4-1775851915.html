7 out of 10 people doing Vibe GTM sound like bots. Here's why. People discover Vibe GTM → get excited → open Claude or some AI tool → type: “Find me 50 SaaS founders and write personalized outreach,” and wonder why nobody replies. Most people blame the prompt but the prompt was never the problem. Vibe GTM only works if you’ve done the hard work first: → A detailed ICP doc that goes beyond job title and company size. Pain points, buying triggers, and the language they actually use. → Your Fireflies call transcripts are stored and structured. Real words from real prospects. How do they describe their problems? What made them say yes? → Your CRM conversations are pulled and organized. What objections came up? What angles were converted? What killed the deal? → All of it is living in specific files that your AI can actually read and reference. Without that, you're just scratching the surface. The AI will write something. It'll read fine. And it'll land in an inbox next to 40 other emails that look exactly the same. The Vibe GTM that actually works starts two weeks before the prompt. Pulling call transcripts. Cleaning up CRM notes. Writing an ICP doc that says something real. Most people skip it because it's boring. P.S. The prompt is 10% of the work. The data infrastructure is 90%.

The biggest mistake you can make in the GCC market?

Treating it as ONE market.

Many sellers say: “We’re selling in the GCC”…
But the reality? Each country plays by completely different rules.

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia: massive volume, high demand — but requires strong call center systems and patience.
🇦🇪 UAE: fast logistics, but intense competition and more demanding customers.
🇰🇼 🇶🇦 Kuwait & Qatar: smaller markets, but very high purchasing power.

Same product ≠ same results.

Where most people go wrong:
They launch the SAME strategy everywhere —
same price, same offer, same confirmation flow…

And end up with unstable results.

In the GCC, success isn’t just about the product.
It’s about adapting EVERYTHING:
→ Offer
→ Pricing
→ Messaging
→ Call center scripts

Once you understand this, things change fast:
Same traffic → higher conversion
Same product → bigger profits

Because you’re no longer guessing…
You’re building a system tailored to each market.

That’s exactly what we do at Lofty Service:
We help brands scale across the GCC with a fully customized COD setup — from call center to delivery & execution.

If you want real results in the GCC:
Understand the market… or work with people who do.

      Amazon just killed the "Steal."🎟️ They changed the coupon display. It used to say: Save 15%. Now it says: Coupon price $53.19. They’ve swapped the percentage for the final price. This is a massive psychological shift. When customers saw a percentage, they didn't do the math. They just saw a "deal" and clicked the green box. That "clip" felt like winning. Now, Amazon is showing the final price upfront. The mystery is gone. The "clipping" sensation is fading. For us sellers, this is a direct hit to conversion. Why? Because logic is the enemy of the impulse buy. $53.19 is just a number. 15% OFF is a feeling. Don't let your listings go cold. If the UI is changing, your strategy has to change. Focus on the value, or the math will kill your margin. Adapt or get left behind.
